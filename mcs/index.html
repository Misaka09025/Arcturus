The Acturus MCS

Modular Cubesat Satellite Solution

Where ideas take flight

The Arcturus MCS (Modular Cube Satellite) is a customizable platform designed to revolutionize space access for small-scale research teams, startups, and academic institutions. Combining affordability, modularity, and user-friendliness, the Arcturus MCS offers unparalleled flexibility and capability for deploying scientific payloads and conducting space research.

Key Features & Advantages

    Allows easy expantion in low cost

  • User-Friendly Interface

    Enables quick get-on-hand

  • High-Performance Payload Bay

    2-6 modules, each of 1.5U

  • Robust Communication Systems

The Arcturus MCS offers a competitive edge to research teams by providing access to advanced space technology and research capabilities that were previously only available to larger institutions with significant financial resources. This democratization of space access helps to level the playing field, enabling more diverse and innovative research to be conducted.

Application Scenarios

The Arcturus MCS is ideal for a wide range of applications, including Earth observation and remote sensing, atmospheric research and weather forecasting, space weather monitoring and prediction, space debris tracking and analysis, and scientific experiments and technology demonstration.
