About Arcturus Aerospace

Arcturus Aerospace is a pioneering company dedicated to providing affordable and accessible CubeSat solutions to customers worldwide. Our mission is to revolutionize the aerospace industry by addressing the high costs associated with designing, constructing, and deploying small-scale satellite platforms.

Company Vision

Arcturus Aerospace is dedicated to democratizing space access by offering modular and customizable cube satellite solutions tailored for small-scale research teams, startups, and academic institutions. Our vision is to empower these entities to conduct groundbreaking scientific research and space exploration, breaking free from traditional cost and complexity barriers. By making space more accessible, we strive to foster innovation, drive technological advancement, and ultimately, expand human understanding and exploration of the cosmos.

Our Innovation

Arcturus Aerospace’s core innovation is a modular CubeSat platform that allows customers to deploy small-scale payloads in orbit in a more affordable, convenient, and less technically demanding manner. Our modular system is based on the CubeSat standard and enables customers to insert their own modules onto the CubeSat and share it with other users, thereby lowering launch costs. Additionally, our CubeSat framework comes with core systems, reducing development and certification time.

Join us at Arcturus Aerospace as we strive to make the aerospace industry more accessible and affordable for all.
